"Brigadeiro De Panela"

A classic Brazilian sweet that is practical and simple to prepare at any time! Check out how to make it now!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk.
  • 4 tablespoons of powdered chocolate or chocolate chips (preferably good quality).
  • 1 tablespoon of butter.

Steps

  1. Open the condensed milk can and empty the contents into a thick-bottomed pan.
  2. Add the chocolate powder (or pieces). Mix well.
  3. Add the butter and heat the mixture over low heat. You have to stir all the time, until you see the mixture come out of the bottom of the pan.
  4. As soon as you reach the point, remove the pan from the heat and place the contents in a heat-resistant dish (if you can, you can grease it with butter in advance), wait for it to cool down and you can consume it.

Enjoy.
