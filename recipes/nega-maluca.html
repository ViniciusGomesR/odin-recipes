"Nega Maluca da Palmirinha"

See how to make this delicious Palmirinha crazy dish: a temptation in terms of practicality and flavor that you can't resist.

Ingredients

CAKE "NEGA MALUCA PALMIRINHA"

  • 2 cups of cocoa powder
  • Â½ cup of coffee diluted in hot water (amount of coffee to taste, a spoon is recommended)
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 2 cups of wheat
  • 1 spoon of yeast

FROSTING "NEGA MALUCA" WITH CHOCOLATE BAR

  • 1 can of cream
  • 200 grams of dark chocolate

Steps

  1. DO NOT make this recipe in a mixer or blender, it must be prepared by hand.
  2. In a bowl, add the oil, eggs, cocoa and sugar, stir well until the eggs are incorporated, add the coffee with hot water.
  3. Stir well.
  4. When it is homogeneous and more liquid, add the sifted wheat and beat by hand.
  5. Add the yeast and stir slowly.
  6. Take everything to a greased shape.
  7. Place the recipe in a preheated oven at 180 degrees for 40 minutes.
  8. Remove and make holes, wait to cool down.
  9. In a saucepan, add the chopped chocolate and cream, stir well until thickened.
  10. Place on top of the cake.
