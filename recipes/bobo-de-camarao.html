"Bobó de Camarão"

A Brazilian cuisine classic prepared in your kitchen: today you'll learn how to make shrimp bobó! This recipe is simple, traditional and is ideal for an unforgettable family lunch!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of fresh shrimp
  • 3 garlic cloves minced and crushed
  • Black pepper
  • 3 onions (1 sliced and 2 grated)
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 bunch of chopped parsley
  • 2 finely chopped green peppers
  • salt
  • 1 lemon juice
  • 1 kg of cassava (prefer those that are already packed and peeled, it is more practical)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 glasses of coconut milk
  • 2 cans of prepared tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of palm oil

Steps

  1. Wash the prawns and season with salt, garlic, pepper and lemon, let it marinate.
  2. Take a pan with water and cook the cassava in small pieces, with bay leaf and sliced onion.
  3. When it's soft, add a glass of coconut milk.
  4. Deixe esfriar um pouco e bata no liquidificador.
  5. Heat the olive oil, add the grated onion and let it brown.
  6. Add the prawns and fry.
  7. Add the 2 cans of pomarola, the green smell, the bell pepper and let it cook for a few minutes.
  8. Junte na mesma panela, a mandioca batida no liquidificador, outro vidro de leite de coco e o azeite de dendê.
  9. Let it boil and it's ready.

Enjoy.
